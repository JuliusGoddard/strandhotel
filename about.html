About The Strand Hotel

Welcome to the Strand Hotel

Boutique Hotel in Bournemouth

Located in the heart of Bournemouth, a stones throw away from the sea

Comfortable Accommodations: The Strand Hotel in Bournemouth offers recently renovated aparthotel rooms with private bathrooms, carpeted floors, and modern amenities including TVs and free WiFi.

Leisure Facilities: Guests can enjoy water sports facilities, a fitness room, and free on-site private parking. Additional amenities include a dining table, work desk, and a seating area.

Prime Location: Located less than 1 km from Boscombe Beach and 9 km from Bournemouth Airport, the hotel is close to attractions such as Bournemouth International Centre (4 km) and Sandbanks (12 km). Guests appreciate the value for money, convenient location, and easy beach access.
